// Most Recent: Meet Ryn — The 'Ghost'

FEBRUARY 22, 2026

She's the one command pretends doesn't exist on paper — a Ghost Operative aboard the Freexyl, specialising in infiltration, espionage, and getting into places she absolutely should not be.

If Fel is the disciplined blade, Ryn is the loose wire. She's chaotic, unpredictable, and treats high-security infiltration like a game she's already winning. She doesn't carry a sidearm — says guns are too loud and too boring. Just claws, bypass tools, and that grin.

Off-duty, she's the one starting card games in the mess hall, giving humans weird nicknames they can't shake, and showing up in places she has no clearance to be — just to prove she can.

// Recent Highlights

RTS Complete Rewrite

  • 200x faster loading — almost instant map loads after full rewrite
  • 60% code reduction — cleaner and more maintainable codebase
  • AI Intelligence System — IQ 0-4 with tactical preferences and aggression scopes. IQ 4 enemies probe your favourites and send feints.
  • Mothership Design — Three motherships (Drone Freighter, Drone Carrier, Research Ship) with modular turrets, power, and field generators
  • Dynamic Grouping — 0.1mt to 100mt group sizes, cruise mode at 3x speed

RPG & World Building

  • All core RPG battle objectives done — 10 playable levels can now be produced rapidly
  • Drunmuire breakthrough — a major creative revelation that ties Amu, Entropy, and Earth together. Scottish Gaelic singer being brought on for the soundtrack.
  • Team decoupling — party members can operate independently with Search & Destroy, Loose Support, or Hold the Fort behaviours
  • Forge content — Soul tanks, extraction chambers, Dark Crystal containment vessels
  • Performance validated — 100 enemies on screen in daylight, no lag

Art & Characters

  • 0% AI art commitment — all AI-generated art being replaced with hand-drawn work
  • FOM soldiers complete with grenades and flashbangs
  • Boss spider walker — Dominion walkers inspired by War of the Worlds tripods
  • Snow assets — cliffs, frozen waterfalls, crystal caves and more
  • Human gunship — for when the Sekarri need humanity to step up
